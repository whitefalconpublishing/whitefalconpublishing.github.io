Setting List Price/MRP

List Price (or MRP) should be greater than the Minimum MRP. You can try different values for List Price greater than the Minimum MRP to come up with an optimal list price.

What is the paper type for the above calculations?

In the above calculations, for black & white interior 70-80 GSM White or Natural-Shade paper is assumed and for colored interiors 130 GSM art paper is assumed.
