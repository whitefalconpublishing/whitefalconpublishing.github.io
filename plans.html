Publishing Plans

You can review our publishing plans and choose the plan that best suits your requirements. To get an accurate estimate for existing plans, please provide us with your book details by completing the form here. You can also create your own custom plan here. We only charge for the services you opt for and publishing the book through our print-on-demand self-publishing platform and other international platforms. Inventory management and order fulfilment is our responsibility. In all our plans, we do not require the author to purchase a certain minimum number of copies. Complimentary copies are provided to the author (which vary in different plans). Author can purchase any number of copies at the author cost price.
