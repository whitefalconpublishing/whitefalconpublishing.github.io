About Competition

Inviting entries under micro-fiction category from school students aged 8-15 years.
This winter vacation let your imagination wander in the literary lanes of your mind to create a story of around 2500 words.

Top 20 entries to be published as an anthology under White Falcon Publishing's imprint.

Each winner to receive 2 copies of the published book and a certificate of appreciation.

Competition Details

Category: Micro-fiction

Genre: Fantasy/Mythology/Thriller

Submission Guidelines

Things you should know

 

What is word limit for submission?

 

Word limit is 2500 words. Entries exceeding this would be penalized.

 

Can multiple submissions be made by one person?

 

Strictly NO. Only one submission per person would be considered per theme. There are two themes, both to be rated separately so you can submit at max. one piece per theme.

How many winners would be decided?

 

Top 20 entries will be shortlisted to be a part of the published book. Each of these 20 finalists will receive ONE (01 copy each) as a gift. Extra hard copies or ebook could be ordered through Amazon, White Falcon’s WFP-Store.

Will an ISBN be assigned to the book of winning entries?

 

Yes. White Falcon Publishing will provide an ISBN to the book.

What will disqualify my submission?

 

Previously published content, plagiarized work or plots containing hate speech, defamatory remarks against political or religious entities will result in immediate disqualification.

Who will own the content rights?

 

If your story is chosen as a finalist then content rights will be reserved by White Falcon Publishing. You can not republish the content anywhere else without permission.

How to submit your story for competition?

 

We require you to submit your stories on ‘WFYWA Submission’ page.

Step 1 :

Open ‘WFYWA Submission’ page and fill your personal details.

Step 2 :

Write your story title, a brief summary and click ‘Choose book file’ button and then browse and select your submission document (MS Word format).

Step 3 :

Review the submission form and then click on ‘Submit’ button to submit your story. Upon successful submission, you will receive a confirmation email.


P.S. Stories once submitted can not be edited. Stories submitted after deadline will not be considered.
