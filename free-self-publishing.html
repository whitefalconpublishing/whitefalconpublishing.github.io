Free Self Publishing

Terms & Conditions

1. Print ready Interior PDF file to be provided by the author that meets the print specifications (guidelines to be shared with the author by us).
2. Only text-only books can be published under the Free publishing plan. You may choose Silver or higher plans for books with images, figures or tables.
3. Add-on services of Basic/Premium Interior Design, Basic/Premium Cover Design, Copy Editing, Proof-Reading and International Distribution do not apply to the Free publishing plan. You may choose Silver or higher plans to avail these services.
4. Availability of the book will be on WFP store. For availability on expanded channels (Amazon, Flipkart, Paytm, ShopClues), Silver or higher plans are applicable.
5. Authors can either provide print ready cover PDF that meets the print specifications or opt for a template based cover design. For reference, click here to view a book with template-based cover made available on WFP Store.
6. ISBN-assignment is included under the Free publishing plan.
7. Under the Free publishing plan, no complimentary copies are provided. Author can purchase any number of copies (minimum order of 25 copies) at the printing cost.
8. Publishing under Free Publishing plan is subject to review of the book content to ensure that it meets our content guidelines. Please refer to our content guidelines here.
